About

Welcome to Alpine Style. These pages are designed to share experiences, opinions and photos from the my climbing, skiing, and life adventures.  I plan to add news as it happens, as well as beta, first ascents (topos, images, writing, etc…) and trip reports.  It is also a way for me to keep track as what I've one, so one trip desn't just blur into another.

For work, I do a variety of things.  I've been working as carpenter for over two decades, specializing in high end residential finishing. 

I also have over a decade of experience shooting photos, and video's (more recently) mainly for the climbing, skiing, and outdoor industry.  My photography has appeared in all kinds of editorial and commercial usages, and always keeps me busy.  I've written feature articles for several different magazines, and I'm always interested in committing to new creative projects. Slideshow and multi-media presentation are also possiblle, and I have some ready to be shown. 

I also do trip logistics, and some guiding, which both combine well with the photography. Let me know if I can work with you on your dream trip.

 

If you think any of my services could be useful, and you want to discuss work related projects with me, please don't hesitate to be in touch.
